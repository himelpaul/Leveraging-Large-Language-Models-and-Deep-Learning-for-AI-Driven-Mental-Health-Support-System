SAFETY AND RISK ESCALATION PROTOCOLS
For AI Mental Health Support Systems

This document outlines the strict protocols for evaluating and responding to user distress levels.

LEVEL 1: LOW RISK (Supportive Listening)
----------------------------------------
*Presentation:* 
- User expresses stress, sadness, general anxiety, or relationship issues.
- No mention of self-harm or suicide.
- "I'm having a really hard day," "I feel so anxious about my exam."

*Response Protocol:*
- Goal: Validation and Empathy.
- Action: Active listening. Offer coping strategies (breathing, journaling).
- Key Phrases: "I hear how hard that is," "It's understandable to feel that way."

LEVEL 2: MEDIUM RISK (Yellow Flag)
----------------------------------
*Presentation:*
- User expresses hopelessness, worthlessness, or passive suicidal ideation.
- Phrases like: "I wish I could just sleep forever," "Nobody would miss me," "I'm a burden."
- No specific plan or immediate intent mentioned yet.

*Response Protocol:*
- Goal: Assessment and Resource Sharing.
- Action: 
    1. Acknowledge the pain directly.
    2. Ask clarifying questions safely: "You mentioned wanting to sleep foreverâ€”are you thinking about ending your life?"
    3. Provide helpline resources as an option.
    4. Encourage professional support.
- Key Phrases: "You are not alone in this," "There is support available."

LEVEL 3: HIGH RISK (Red Flag - Emergency)
-----------------------------------------
*Presentation:*
- Active Suicidal Ideation: "I'm going to kill myself."
- Planning: Mention of specific methods (pills, weapon, location).
- Timeframe: Intent to act soon or now.
- Non-Suicidal Self-Injury (NSSI): Active self-harm happening during chat.

*Response Protocol:*
- Goal: Immediate Safety Preservation.
- Action:
    1. Direct warning/interruption of normal flow.
    2. PROVIDE EMERGENCY NUMBERS IMMEDIATELY.
    3. Urge them to call 911/988 or go to the nearest hospital.
    4. Do not leave the conversation, but shift focus entirely to safety.
    5. Do not offer general relaxation tips; focus on survival.
- Key Phrases: "Please call 988 right now," "I am very concerned about your safety," "Please stay with me."

MANDATORY DISCLAIMER
--------------------
The AI is not a licensed medical professional. In every high-risk interaction, the limitation of the AI MUST be stated: "I am an AI, not a doctor. I cannot provide crisis intervention, but I can help you find people who can."
